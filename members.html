Ferdinand Kiplanagat

Hello my name is Ferdinand. I come from the Northern part of Kenya which is really dry. This made me think about environment conservation since water and other survival resources were scarce in my home area. I came up with ideas and involved my 3 friends. We formed a website and so far we have held 4 events and are still looking forward to holding more so we can come with enough funds to conserve the environment. I am the president of Tech for Environment Conservation

Meshack Kimutai

In one of the conferences I attended with him, we came to know about technology for impact. He came up with the environment idea and I was thrilled since it was something I had been pondering about. Ivy Mureithi

Hi guys, I am Ivy. I have always been interested in tree planting since the days of the late Wangari Maathai. I come from the Coast Region of Kenya. I was saddened by the fact that coconut trees are almost becoming extinct. I made it my personal initiative to always plant a tree wherever I went, but after some time it became devastating since the trees were not taken care of and most dried. I later enrolled to Moringa School where I joined hands with my current partners who we shared similar passion for environment conservation. Tech for Environment Conservation was born and I immediately got on board.Techonology is a powerful tool in environmental conservation awareness. Currently I am the secretary of the group.
